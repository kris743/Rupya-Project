# 🌱 Rupiya.app

**Residue-free Farming • Carbon Credits • Rural Finance**

Rupiya.app is a modern agri-tech platform designed to empower farmers with sustainable farming practices and climate-positive income opportunities. It combines AI, satellite data, and fintech solutions to increase yield, reduce costs, and unlock additional income through carbon credits.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Rupiya.app provides an all-in-one digital ecosystem for farmers, FPOs, buyers, and investors. The platform focuses on promoting residue-free farming while enabling farmers to earn from verified carbon credits.

---

## 🌍 Key Features

### 🛰️ Satellite Soil Testing

* Remote soil analysis using high-resolution satellite data
* Insights on soil nutrients and soil organic carbon (SOC)

### 🤖 AI Crop Monitoring

* NDVI and multispectral analytics
* Crop health tracking, disease detection, and yield forecasting

### 🌾 Agronomy Advisory

* Data-driven recommendations
* On-ground agronomist support

### 🌿 Residue-free Inputs

* 35+ eco-friendly farming inputs
* Organic, neem-based, and certified solutions

### 💰 Carbon Credits

* Earn additional income through regenerative farming
* 1 carbon credit = 1 tonne CO₂ reduced
* Transparent verification and payouts
